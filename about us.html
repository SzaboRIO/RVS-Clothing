About Us


RVS is more than just a clothing brand. It’s the result of friendship, determination, and a passion for creativity.

Founded by three friends, Gonçalo Rio, Ana Vale and Licínio Sousa, who met during a software development course, RVS reflects the perfect blend of innovation and fashion.

The name RVS comes from the first letters of our founders' last names: Rio, Vale and Sousa, a testament to the bond that brought this vision to life. What began as an idea shared between friends has grown into a brand dedicated to offering stylish, high-quality clothing for those who value individuality and self-expression.

At RVS, we are inspired by the intersection of technology and design, bringing the same attention to detail and problem-solving mindset we learned as developers into the fashion world. Every collection we create is designed to be modern, versatile, and timeless, with a focus on quality and sustainability.

We believe that clothing should empower you to be your best self. Whether you’re dressing for work, play, or something in between, RVS has something for every moment.

Welcome to RVS—where friendship and innovation come together to redefine fashion.
